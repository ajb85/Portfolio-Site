A Little About Me

I come from a background in restaurant management ranging from running QSRs myself, training and developing management teams, and up to overseeing up to just over 30 stores. This is where I first cut my teeth with programming, as overseeing so many restaurants and trying to give detailed feedback on numbers is very time-consuming.

I taught myself just enough Visual Basic in Excel to create a macro that could analyze any number of spreadsheets and compile a summary of a store's performance. Armed with that, I was able to discuss issues with my managers and help them lower their operation costs. Using this tool, I was able to drop food cost at some of my problem stores by over 4% (~$50k/yr)! I learned then programming can be a fun, challenging, and effective way to tackle problems.
