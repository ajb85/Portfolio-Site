The Diner is a text-based, perma-death game built in React with an integrated chat lobby. Players connect, give their name, pick from randomly generated stats, and then join the chat lobby. Once in the lobby, players can communicate with each other normally or with "/me" emotes. Players can challenge each other to a fight, with the loser being ejected from the app.
